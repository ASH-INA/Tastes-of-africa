North Africa

North African cuisine is a unique blend of Arab, Berber, and Mediterranean influences. The food is famous for its use of aromatic spices such as cumin, coriander, saffron, and paprika. Key ingredients include couscous, lamb, chicken, and vegetables like eggplant and tomatoes. Traditional cooking techniques include slow-cooking and braising in tagines (traditional clay pots).
