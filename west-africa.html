West Africa

West African cuisine is vibrant and diverse, influenced by a variety of cultures and traditions. The food is known for its use of bold, spicy ingredients, such as chilies, ginger, and garlic. Common ingredients include rice, cassava, yams, and beans. Cooking methods vary by region, but many dishes are prepared by grilling, boiling, and frying.
